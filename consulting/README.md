# Glytec GTM Consulting — Project Overview

## What We're Building

An **AI-powered Sales Development Representative (SDR) infrastructure** for Glytec's go-to-market team, targeting hospital systems for their insulin management platform (Glucommander / Intelligent Glycemic Management).

## The Problem

Glytec has a massive addressable market ($810M across 1,683 parent health systems) but limited SDR coverage:

- **73% of facilities have no assigned rep** (3,995 of 5,475 in the supplemental list)
- Only **54 of 1,683 parent systems** (3.2%) are current clients
- The unassigned pool skews urban and includes large Enterprise/Strategic accounts — the highest-value segment
- Named reps are concentrated on rural Critical Access Hospitals, leaving the biggest opportunities untouched

Manual outreach cannot scale to cover this whitespace. The AI SDR closes that gap.

## What We're Delivering

### Infrastructure (v1–v4, iterative releases)

A progressively enhanced outbound system that includes:

1. **Contact enrichment pipeline** — Identify and enrich 15+ contacts per account across 66 ICP titles (CEO, CNO, CQO, CMIO, CFO, Chief of Endocrinology, etc.), reaching 50% coverage by v1 and 80%+ by v2
2. **Outbound campaign engine** — Automated, persona-targeted outreach using the Magnetic Messaging Framework, segmented by EHR type, financial health, and segment
3. **Reporting & analytics** — Pipeline visibility, campaign performance, and account coverage metrics
4. **Process documentation & handoff** — Training materials, runbooks, and executive report-out for Glytec's internal team

### Campaign Strategy

- **Segment prioritization:** Enterprise/Strategic first (296 accounts, $277M opp, avg $937K deal) → Commercial (1,329 accounts, $102M)
- **Persona sequencing:** Open with CNO/CQO (intent 95/90) → expand to CMIO, CFO, Chief of Endocrinology → activate influencers (hospitalists, pharmacists, diabetes specialists)
- **EHR-based messaging:** Epic shops get "DIY calculator liability" angle; Cerner/MEDITECH get integration/modernization angles
- **Urgency levers:** CMS glycemic eCQM mandates, financial distress (55.9% of facilities unprofitable), readmission rates
